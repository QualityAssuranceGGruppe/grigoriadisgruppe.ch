Policy - §01sub01PMG Scope of the Firm

Purpose: To define the scope of the Firm’s activities, investment focus, and strategic boundaries.

Scope: Applies to all strategic, investment, partnership, and operational decisions.

Policy Statement: The Firm may invest in smaller companies, businesses, and specialist talents without limitation, provided visible profit potential exists.

Investment Scope:

  • Investment in early-stage or small-scale companies
  • Investment in individual specialist talents and expertise
  • Participation in diverse industries, sectors, and business themes
  • Engagement in opportunities across all markets and regions

Investment Criteria:

  • Clearly identifiable profit opportunity
  • Commercial viability and value creation potential
  • Align with risk appetite defined by Managing Director
  • Comply with legal/regulatory requirements

Governance & Oversight: Final approval rests with Managing Director. Decisions documented for transparency.

Review & Continuous Evaluation: Periodic review to ensure relevance and effectiveness.

Policy - §01sub02PMG Managing Policies

Purpose: Ensure effective planning, operation, control, and oversight of departments.

Scope: Applies to all departments; governs leaders, documentation, reporting.

Roles & Responsibilities:

  • CEO ensures resources for Board of Directors.
  • Managing Director appoints/approves supervisors, controls documentation, reviews reports.
  • Key Account Manager ensures customer/business partner contact.
  • Supervisors lead departments, ensure compliance, report performance and risks.
  • Financial Advisor oversees market shares and investments, reports quarterly.

Documented Info & Process Control: ISO 9001:2015 Clause 7.5; info approved, updated, accessible.

Compliance & Monitoring: Mandatory; corrective actions for noncompliance.

Related ISO 9001 Clauses: 5.3, 7.5, 8.1, 9.1, 10.2

Policy - §01sub03PMG Partnership Duties

Contracts must show profit, benefits, and growth; clear terms; resources provided; standard partnership investing.
