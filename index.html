§01sub01 Scope

GRIGORIADIS GRUPPE operates as a private family wealth management entity dedicated exclusively to its members. No fees are charged for any services provided.

The firm focuses on the preservation and strategic growth of capital through disciplined, low-risk investment strategies.

Investments are allocated across diversified assets and global markets, ensuring stability and long-term value creation.

Monthly withdrawals are predefined and proportionally structured based on the number of active members utilizing the service.
